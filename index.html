Privacy Policy for Instagram Auto-Reply Service

Last Updated: July 17, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Instagram Auto-Reply Service ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our automated messaging service on Instagram.

2. Information We Collect

When you send a direct message (DM) to our Instagram account, we may collect:

  • Message Content: The text of your message
  • User ID: An anonymous Instagram identifier
  • Timestamps: When messages are sent/received

3. How We Use Your Data

The collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Generate automated replies to your messages
  • Improve response accuracy
  • Monitor service performance

4. Data Storage & Security

We do not permanently store your messages. Data is:

  • Processed in real-time through n8n automation platform
  • Deleted immediately after replying
  • Protected with industry-standard security measures

5. Third-Party Services

The Bot relies on:

  • Instagram/Facebook API (message delivery)
  • n8n (automation workflow)

6. User Rights

You may:

  • Stop interacting with the Bot at any time
  • Request deletion of your past messages
  • Disable DMs with our Instagram account

Updates will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date.
